Nertivia's Features
We've added many unique useful features to Nertivia. Here are some of them!
Quoting Messages

Are you having a big conversation with lots of people? On Nertivia, you can easily quote other users to show who's talking to who.

Theming

Unlike most chatting platforms, Nertivia allows for custom theming using CSS, a commonly used styling language. You can create your own themes, or use other user's themes in the Explore tab.

Explore

Nertivia provides an Explore tab to users so they can find servers, themes, emojis, you name it. And it's all curated by Nertivia's staff to ensure safety and security.
